About Us

Empowering Youth with Practical IT and Digital Skills.

Arfa Kareem Institute Kotli is dedicated to providing high-quality, skill-based education in the fields of IT, digital marketing, graphic design, web development, and more. Named in honor of Pakistan’s youngest IT prodigy, Arfa Karim, our mission is to empower students with modern skills that meet the demands of today’s digital world. With experienced instructors, hands-on training, and a commitment to excellence, we aim to build a tech-savvy generation ready to lead and innovate.
